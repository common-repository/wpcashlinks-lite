Convert your keywords into cash generating links and easily create a silo type link structure.

== Description ==
WPCashLinks LITE allows you to convert your keywords into cash links by easily linking them with your affiliate links or other cash generating links. Also you can very easily create a silo type link structure by interlinking your inner pages in just minutes.

Contextual links are very important these days and they are the most powerful links to be exact. You can easily link to your favorite sites from your content using this simple and easy plugin. This plugin makes it easy to manage these interlinking so you don\'t have to edit every post or page on your site.

Features:
*   Easily link your keywords with proper pages
*   Great for inner site link creation

== Installation ==
To install follow this steps:

1. Upload `wpcashlinks-lite` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the \'Plugins\' menu in WordPress
